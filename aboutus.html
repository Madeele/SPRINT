About Us

Freshly made Italian ice cream on stick.

The essence of our ice cream sticks is, of course, the fresh ice cream made on milk. Italian ice cream has a lower air content and is much less greasy than normal industrial ice sticks. It gives Italian ice cream a stronger flavor and a softer texture.

Coated with quality chocolate.

We dip most of our ice cream in either white chocolate, milk chocolate or dark chocolate. And sometimes we even color our white chocolate with strawberry juice to make it pink. Or with pistachio to make it green.

Turned into goodies.

Finally, before we put the ice sticks out in the freezer counter of our store, we put the ice cream with still liquid chocolate into hazelnuts, almonds, coconut, crunchy or a fifth type of crumb. And then we have an unparalleled good ice stick.
